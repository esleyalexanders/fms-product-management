About CleanCo

Your trusted partner in professional cleaning services

Our Story

Founded in 2015, CleanCo has grown from a small local business to Australia's leading cleaning service provider.

What started as a passion for creating clean, healthy environments has evolved into a comprehensive cleaning service that serves hundreds of satisfied clients across Australia. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Today, we're proud to offer a wide range of cleaning services, from residential homes to commercial buildings, with a team of over 50 experienced professionals who share our vision of delivering exceptional results.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional cleaning services that exceed our clients' expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and recognized cleaning service provider in Australia, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, respect, and sustainability guide everything we do. We believe in treating every space and every client with the care they deserve.
